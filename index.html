The importance of early childhood conversations

Several multi-decade studies show conversations between parent and child at early ages remarkably contribute to the the child's critical thinking as well as cognitive, emotional and linguistic development, at later stages of life.

How does it work?

Everyday, you receive a few conversation starters formed around one specific word. Each word is selected intentionally to grow your child in one specific child development aspect, such as linguistic, social, cognitive and emotional development.
